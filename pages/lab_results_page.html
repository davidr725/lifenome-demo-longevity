The Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) is a group of 14 blood tests that provides important information about your body's chemical balance and metabolism. This panel examines your kidney function, liver function, blood sugar levels, and electrolyte and fluid balance.

Healthcare providers commonly order this test as part of routine health screenings, to monitor chronic conditions like diabetes or kidney disease, or to check how medications might be affecting your organs. The results help assess your overall health and can detect early signs of various medical conditions.
