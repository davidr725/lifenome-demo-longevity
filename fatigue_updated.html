Fatigue

Understanding Fatigue

Fatigue is more than just feeling tired‚Äîit's a persistent lack of energy that can significantly impact daily life and long-term health. When someone experiences ongoing fatigue, the body may be signaling deeper issues that affect cellular energy production, hormone balance, or recovery processes, all of which are crucial for maintaining vitality as we age.

Multiple factors can contribute to fatigue, including sleep quality, nutrient deficiencies, stress levels, and underlying health conditions. Genetic markers can influence how efficiently the body produces and uses energy, while lifestyle choices like physical activity, diet, and stress management play major roles in determining energy levels day to day.
